Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Piñas - IT campus

VISION

To develop human resources that are imbued with proper values, responsive to the needs of the community and the capability to cope with the fast-changing world where knowledge in Information reign supreme.

MISSION

To give employment to 1,000 graduates for the first year and eventually increasing by 2,000 graduates at the end of the fifth year by producing graduates widely admired for their professional competence, innovative skills, dedicated to work, integrity, good values and the right attitudes in the practice of their profession.

Message from our beloved

Mayor

Greetings to our beloved Gold batch of Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Information Technology Training Institute (DFCAITTI) on occasion of your commencement exercises!

Your graduation theme, saktong buhay: Sa Dekalidad na Edukasyon Pinanday, summarizes my vision to see every DFCAITTI graduate realizes his dream of having a quality education for a better life. I am happy to witness your success with the help of quality public education and trainings we provide for you. How you fare in exceedingly competitive world depends largely on how you are educated and trained in our learning institutions.

Rest assured of steadfast commitment to provide every Las Pinas free and quality education to prepare you become more productive and responsible citizens. This is the legacy i wish to give everyone who never stopped believing that we all deserve a chance at free and quality education.

Congratulations and Mabuhay!
