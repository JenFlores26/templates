Your passport to a better life. 

Greetings to our beloved Gold Batch of Dr, Felimon C. Aguilar Information Technology Training Institute (DFCAITTI) on the occasion of your commencement exercises!. Your graduation theme, Saktong Buhay: Sa Dekalidad na Edukasyon Pinanday, summarize my vision to see every DFCAITTI graduate realizes his dream of having a quality education for better life.

VISION

To develop human resources that are imbued with proper values, responsive to the needs of the community and the capability to cope with fast-changing world where knowledge in Information Technology reigns supreme

MiSSION

To give employment to 1,000 graduates for the first year and eventually increasing by 2,000 graduates at the end of the fifth year by producing competence, innovative skills, dedication to work, integrity, good values and the right attitudes in the practice of their profession.
